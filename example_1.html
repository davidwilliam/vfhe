FHE Scheme

For illustrative purposes only.
Plaintext space

$\mathbb{Z}_{q} = \{0,\ldots,q-1\}$

Ciphertext space

$\mathbb{Z}_{n} = \{0,\ldots,n-1\}$

Key Generation

Arbitrarily choose $p,q,r$

Encryption

Given $m \in \mathbb{Z}_{q}$ and the arbitrarily defined $p,q,r$,

uniformly choose $d \leftarrow \{1,\ldots,r-1\}$

and compute

$c = m + d + qp \bmod n$

Due to the uniform values of $d$, the encryption is probabilistic and for each message $m$ there are $r-1$ possible ciphertexts.

Decryption

$m = c \bmod q$

Addition

$c_3 = c_1 + c_2 \bmod n$

Multiplication

$c_3 = c_1 c_2 \bmod n$
